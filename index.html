Our mission

Democracy runs on people power.

VolunteerMe is a powerful platform designed to connect passionate volunteers with the political candidates and community leaders they support. Our platform helps organize volunteer efforts during election campaigns by providing schedules, reminders, and automated phone calls so volunteers always know when and where they are needed. Whether it's knocking on doors, supporting outreach programs, or assisting local candidates — from city council races to mayoral campaigns — VolunteerMe makes it easy to stay informed, stay organized, and make a real difference in your community. Because the leaders who fight for your neighborhood deserve a team that shows up.

How it works

From sign-up to showing up — in minutes.

No complicated apps, no confusion. VolunteerMe handles the scheduling and reminders so you can focus on what matters: making a difference.

Features

Everything a great volunteer needs.

Built for real campaigns, real volunteers, and real impact — with the tools to keep everyone in sync.

📞

Automated reminder calls

Personalized phone calls remind you of upcoming volunteer shifts the day before and morning of. No app required — just pick up.

💬

Text message alerts

SMS notifications include the location, time, and what to bring — sent right to your phone so you're always prepared.

🗺️

Shift scheduling & maps

See your full volunteer schedule in one place with integrated maps showing exactly where to meet your team.

✅

Check-in & attendance

Campaigns track turnout in real time, so coordinators always know who's on the ground and who needs a nudge.
